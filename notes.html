Chapter 1: Cell Biology

Topic 1: Cell Structure

The cell is the basic structural and functional unit of life.

A cell is the smallest living unit capable of performing all vital functions.

Cells contain specialized structures called organelles.

Organelles are membrane-bound structures within cells that perform specific tasks.

Topic 2: Cell Functions

Cells help in growth, metabolism, and repair of tissues.

Chapter 2: Enzymes

Topic 1: What are Enzymes?

Enzymes are biological catalysts that speed up chemical reactions.

A biological catalyst is a substance that increases reaction rate without being consumed.

Topic 2: Specificity of Enzymes

Enzymes show substrate specificity.

Substrate specificity means an enzyme acts only on a particular substrate.
