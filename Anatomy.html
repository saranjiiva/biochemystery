Hanging vs Strangulation
Forensic Differentiation – Medicolegal Perspective

Neck Anatomy (Forensic Relevance)

Common Injury Sites:

  • Hyoid fracture (common in strangulation)
  • Thyroid cartilage damage
  • Neck muscle hemorrhage
