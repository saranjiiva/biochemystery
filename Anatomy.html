Definitions

Hanging: Constriction of neck by ligature where body weight acts as constricting force.

Strangulation: Constriction of neck by external force without suspension.

Mechanism of Death

  • Asphyxia
  • Cerebral ischemia
  • Vagal inhibition
  • Cervical spine injury (judicial hanging)
