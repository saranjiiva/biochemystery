ANAPLEROSIS | Minor Pathway Entry

Propionyl-CoA → Succinyl-CoA Pathway

1
Propionyl-CoA Carboxylase

Carboxylation

Biotin (B7) + ATP

Propionyl-CoA (3C) accepts CO₂.
Requires ATP hydrolysis.
Product: D-Methylmalonyl-CoA

2
Methylmalonyl-CoA Racemase

Racemization

No Cofactor

Stereoisomer conversion.
D-isomer converted to L-isomer.
Product: L-Methylmalonyl-CoA

3
Methylmalonyl-CoA Mutase

Rearrangement

B12 (Cobalamin)

Carbon skeleton rearrangement.
Requires 5'-Deoxyadenosylcobalamin.
Product: Succinyl-CoA (Enter TCA)

!
Clinical Correlation

Methylmalonic Acidemia

Defect: Mutase or B12

Blockage causes methylmalonic acid accumulation.
Symptoms: Acidosis, developmental delay.
Enters urine (diagnostic).

Odd-Chain Fatty Acids

Valine, Isoleucine, Methionine, Threonine

VOMIT Pathway Mnemonic

Critical B12 Requirement

One of only two B12-dependent reactions in humans
