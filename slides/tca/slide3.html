Matrix (TCA Cycle)
  • The entire TCA cycle occurs in the mitochondrial matrix.
  • Pyruvate dehydrogenase complex (PDH) is also located in the matrix.
  • Succinate dehydrogenase is the only enzyme embedded in the inner mitochondrial membrane.
  • SDH also functions as Complex II of the electron transport chain.
  • NADH and FADH₂ produced enter the electron transport chain for ATP synthesis.
