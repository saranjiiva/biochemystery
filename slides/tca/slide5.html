E1: Pyruvate Dehydrogenase

First enzyme of the Pyruvate Dehydrogenase Complex

Reaction

Pyruvate → Hydroxyethyl-TPP + CO₂
  • E1 catalyzes the oxidative decarboxylation of pyruvate.
  • It is the rate-limiting step of the PDH complex.
  • The enzyme requires the coenzyme Thiamine pyrophosphate (TPP).
Cofactor: TPP (active form of Vitamin B₁)
