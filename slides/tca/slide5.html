E1: Pyruvate Dehydrogenase

Enzyme Component 1 of PDH Complex
Cofactor Required

Thiamine Pyrophosphate (TPP)

Reaction: Oxidative Decarboxylation

Substrate Pyruvate (3 Carbons)
Product Hydroxyethyl-TPP + CO₂ Release

"Rate-limiting step of the PDH complex"

1. Ionization

TPP thiazolium ring loses a proton to form a carbanion (Ylide).

2. Nucleophilic Attack

Carbanion attacks the carbonyl carbon of pyruvate.

3. Decarboxylation

CO₂ leaves; the remaining acetyl group is attached to TPP.

Focus: TPP Structure

Vitamin B1 Derivative (Thiamine)

Clinical Correlation: Thiamine deficiency (Beriberi/Wernicke-Korsakoff) impairs E1 function, leading to lactic acidosis due to pyruvate accumulation.
