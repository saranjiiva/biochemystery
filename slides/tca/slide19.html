Key Concepts

Thermodynamics

The reaction is strongly endergonic (positive ΔG).

It proceeds forward only because the product Oxaloacetate is immediately consumed by the highly exergonic Citrate Synthase reaction (Step 1), keeping [OAA] extremely low.

Regeneration

Regenerates oxaloacetate to allow the cycle to restart. This step completes the oxidation of the carbon skeleton derived from acetyl-CoA.
