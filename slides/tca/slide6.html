E2: Dihydrolipoyl Transacetylase

Second enzyme of the Pyruvate Dehydrogenase Complex (Core enzyme)

Reaction

Hydroxyethyl-TPP + CoA → Acetyl-CoA + Reduced lipoamide
  • E2 transfers the acetyl group to Coenzyme A.
  • Forms the high-energy thioester bond of acetyl-CoA.
  • Contains a flexible lipoamide swinging arm.
Cofactors: Lipoic acid (lipoamide) and Coenzyme A (Vitamin B₅ derivative)
