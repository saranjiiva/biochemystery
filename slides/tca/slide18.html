Clinical Correlations

Fumarase Deficiency

Rare autosomal recessive disorder causing severe early-onset metabolic crisis.

  • Severe encephalopathy & seizures
  • Increased urinary fumarate

Tumor Suppression

Fumarase acts as a tumor suppressor. Mutations are associated with HLRCC (Hereditary Leiomyomatosis and Renal Cell Cancer).
