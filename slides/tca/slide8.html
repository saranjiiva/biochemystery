METABOLISM | Pyruvate Dehydrogenase Complex

Sequential Reaction Mechanism

1
E1: Pyruvate DH

Decarboxylation

TPP

Pyruvate attacks TPP.
CO₂ released.
Hydroxyethyl-TPP formed.

2
E1 → E2

Oxidation & Transfer

Lipoamide

Hydroxyethyl group oxidized to acetyl.
Transferred to lipoamide (swinging arm).
S-S bond breaks.

3
E2: Transacetylase

Formation of Product

CoA-SH

Acetyl group transferred from lipoamide to CoA.
Acetyl-CoA produced.

4
E3: Dihydrolipoyl DH

Reoxidation of Lipoamide

FAD

Reduced lipoamide transfers electrons to FAD.
Forms FADH₂.
Lipoamide regenerated.

5
E3: Dihydrolipoyl DH

Electron Output

NAD⁺

FADH₂ transfers electrons to NAD⁺.
NADH + H⁺ produced.
Enzyme reset.
