Clinical Case – Apply Your Knowledge
A 19-year-old male presents with polyuria, polydipsia, abdominal pain and deep rapid breathing. He appears dehydrated and confused.
Lab Findings:

• Blood Glucose: 480 mg/dL
• Serum Ketones: Positive
• pH: 7.12
• HCO₃⁻: Low
Diagnosis:
Diabetic Ketoacidosis (DKA)
Absolute Insulin Deficiency → Lipolysis → Ketogenesis → Metabolic Acidosis
