Hanging vs Strangulation
Comprehensive Forensic & Medicolegal Differentiation

1. Definition

Hanging: Constriction of neck by ligature where body weight acts as the constricting force.

Strangulation: Constriction of neck by external force without suspension.

2. Pathophysiology

  • Airway compression → Asphyxia
  • Carotid artery compression → Cerebral ischemia
  • Jugular venous obstruction → Congestion
  • Vagal inhibition → Sudden cardiac arrest
  • Cervical spine injury (judicial hanging)
